About Us

Coronavirus has put the entire world at a halt. The death-toll is rising gradually. Even our beloved nation, Bangladesh, is struggling to tackle the issue. Over 20% of our population is poverty stricken, that is more 35 million people. There is a higher chance of these people dying from hunger snd starvation rather than by the virus itself if people like you and me don’t step forward. With the hopes of getting voluntary support from everyone, Helping Hand - Habiganj was founded. Our aim is to provide food and supplies to the needy. Apart from providing food, our free telemedicine service, conducted by our doctors, is open 24 hours. For providing technical support, renowned internet service provider Lights has come forward. For logistics and distribution support ‘Amar Bazar Habiganj’ a local online grocery service is helping in this initiative.

About Us

Coronavirus has put the entire world at a halt. The death-toll is rising gradually. Even our beloved nation, Bangladesh, is struggling to tackle the issue. Over 20% of our population is poverty stricken, that is more 35 million people. There is a higher chance of these people dying from hunger snd starvation rather than by the virus itself if people like you and me don’t step forward. With the hopes of getting voluntary support from everyone, Helping Hand - Habiganj was founded. Our aim is to provide food and supplies to the needy. Apart from providing food, our free telemedicine service, conducted by our doctors, is open 24 hours. For providing technical support, renowned internet service provider Lights has come forward. For logistics and distribution support ‘Amar Bazar Habiganj’ a local online grocery service is helping in this initiative.
